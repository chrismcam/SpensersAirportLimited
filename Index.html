Our Services

  • Flight Management

    Track and record all flight information, including arrivals, departures, cargo, passengers (international, domestic, in-transit), status (departed, arrived, delayed, cancelled), and flight duration.

  • Crew Management

    Maintain a comprehensive database of crew members, including pilots, flight attendants, their qualifications, employment details, contact information, and assigned flights.

  • Aircraft Management

    Store detailed information about all aircraft in the fleet, including registration number, owner, capacity (seating and cargo), maximum takeoff weight, and aircraft type.

  • Airport Management

    Maintain details about arrival and destination airports, including name, city, and country.
